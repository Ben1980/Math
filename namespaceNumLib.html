A numerical integration method using the trapezoidal approach.

TrapezoidalIntegration is using the trapezoidal approach to acomplish numerical integrations as described at thoughts-on-coding.com and calculates the integral of function \(f\) in range \([a-b]\).

\[ w=\frac{b-a}{N} \]

\[ x_i=a+i \cdot w \]

\[ x_{i+1}=a+\left( i+1 \right) \cdot w \]

\[ I=\textstyle \sum_{0}^{N}\frac{x_{i+1}-x_i}{2}\left( f(x_i)+f(x_{i+1}) \right) \]
